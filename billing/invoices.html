Send Invoices

BILLING > Send Invoices

We can send receipts and invoices through the customer card by email or snail mail for any service that the customer has ever received.

ðŸ’¸ For Receipts of Individual Invoices

  1. Go to the Invoice tab
  2. From the left hand side, select the charge that the customer wants a receipt for
  3. After selecting the specific charge, select "Actions" in the top right
  4. Select "Email Receipt"
  5. Confirm that the email address on the Info tab is correct
  6. Click "Send Email"

Important Note: If you hit "Send Email," the email will send right away, so it is important to make sure that the email is correct before sending.

Alternative: Snail Mail

  • You can also send the Invoice through "Snail Mail"
  • Leave notes in the account documenting what was sent

Email/Snail Mail for Date Range

Agent Reminders

  • Always verify email address before sending receipts
  • Document in account notes when invoices/receipts are sent
  • For multiple invoices, recommend Customer Portal first
  • Email receipts send immediately - double-check before clicking send
  • Both email and snail mail options are available for all invoice types
