Stop Collection Calls/Texts

Agents have the ability to add customers to the "Remove From Collections Texts and Calls" spreadsheet list when a customer requests. This process ensures that customers who no longer wish to receive collection-related communications via text and call are respected.

Guidelines for Agents

  • Any agent can add a customer to the list when the customer explicitly requests to stop receiving texts and calls regarding collections
  • Agents should not proactively offer this opt-out option
  • It should only be offered if the customer expresses frustration or requests it
  • They will still owe the balance left on the account but this will assure they do not receive calls or texts in the future concerning their bill

Customers added to this list will still receive letters and emails regarding collections. There is no legal obligation to allow customers to opt out of emails; thus, they will continue to receive email communications.

How to Add to the List

  1. Access the List: Click the link to the "Remove From Collections Texts and Calls" document
  2. Scroll to the Bottom: Navigate to the bottom of the list
  3. Enter Customer Information: In the soonest available cell, add the following details:
    • Account Number
    • Date Added
    • Reason for Removal
    • If any balance was paid
    • Balance age
    • Potential date to add back to dialers

Agent Reminders

  • Only add customers who explicitly request to stop receiving calls/texts
  • Do not offer this option proactively
  • Customer will still receive letters and emails
  • Account balance remains due despite opting out
  • Document all required information in the spreadsheet
  • Leave notes in the customer account about the opt-out request
